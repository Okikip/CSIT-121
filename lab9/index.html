The Woman King

Action & Adventure

Main actors

  1. Viola Davis as Nanisca
  2. Lashana Lynch as Izogie
  3. Thuso Mbedu as Nawi
  4. John Boyega as Krol Ghezo

Captivating part of the movie

  • The colors of the movie were captivating
  • The action and the emotions portrayed with the action
  • The accent being so accurate and sounding like its actual Africans made it so believable
  • The camera shots were so cinematic, the reveal shots, the close up shots and the details brought everything together

My review for this movie The Woman King would be a 10/10. The reason why I said this was because the way it was written, you could tell the writer studied Nollywood movies, which are movies produced by African countries just like how Hollywood is American. Also, the plot and character development are beautiful. The character development of Nawi, a young girl who is adopted and given away, she is later in a position where she meets her mother and they go to war together, that is a great storyline and it was the main part of the whole movie and kept me wanting to know more.
