Jollof Rice

The best rice you will ever have

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Vegetable Oil

Recipe

  1. Pour the oil in an empty pot and turn the heat on
  2. When the oil is heated add the tomato paste and stir till it's all melted
  3. During the process get the rice and wash it off with water
  4. Slice the onions and put it in the heated pot with the oil and tomato paste keep stirring
  5. Five minutes after pour water then add salt, thyme, and curry
  6. When you are satisfied with the taste of the water then now add the rice
  7. Let the rice boil and stir it softly during the process
  8. Wait till it's ready and the rice feels edible
  9. Then you have your JOLLOF RICE

Why Jollof rice is my favorite recipe

The reason Why Jollof Rice is my favorite recipe is because it is a national treasure for my country Nigeria and I take pride in my culture, so me knowing how to prepare it is really an honor to me and my family.
